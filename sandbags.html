Sandbags

How to Use

  • Lay down plastic sheeting (shower curtain liner, tarp) and stack bags on top of each other to minimize flood damage.

  • Fill bags 1/2 to 3/4 full as overfilling makes them difficult to stack and creates gaps in sandbag wall.

  • If sandbags are dry store them in a high, dry place for future storms.
